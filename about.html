About Us

We are E-waste Grinders

We at E-waste Grinders work on Channelinzing the incoming and outgoing of the scrap across the globe. We strongly belive that the E-waste Vendoring industry has a huge potential when it comes to employment generation and contributing to the GDP of every nation.
Our vision is to collect and reuse the e-waste. Our model also provides a job security to poor scrap vendors and channelize the flow of e-waste scrap in all the nations in a more orderly fashion. We take in the customer pickup requests and send our vendors to collect the scrap. We are here to push the idea of recycling and reusing to new heights by bridging the gap between collectors, buyers and companies.

Join us in our journey on making the world a better and cleaner place.

""Recycling more e-waste scraps can help local businesses expand jobs while supporting the goals of sustainability, in addition to that e-waste are the most dangerous and hazardous substances for our planet to dump it as it is one of the greatest sin a Mankind can commit on mother Earth.""

What we do?

We the E-waste Grinders focus on channelling the scrap flow all over Globe without effecting the existing traditional scrap mentoring system. We strongly believe that the e-waste scrap industry holds huge potential when it comes to sustainable business models.The idea is Simple, we create a platform that connects the three most important entities in the Scrap collecting industry. We create a network where we take in the commoner’s details who’s willing to sell the scrap and we contact the scrap collectors for collecting all the scrap for us.

Why manufacture more when we can reuse the components?
Why exploit new resources when we can recycle it from e-waste generated?
